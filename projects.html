Project 03
System Dynamics Modelling
Systems Thinking & Simulation
Using system dynamics to model the UTAMU student enrollment system and simulate the impact of policy interventions over 10 years
What I Did
  • Identified 3 feedback loops — R1 Reputation Growth, B1 Capacity Constraint, B2 Dropout Reputation
  • Built causal loop diagram in Visual Paradigm
  • Built stock and flow model in STELLA
  • Ran Base, Scenario A, and Scenario B simulations
Impact
  • Scenario A (retention) outperforms Scenario B (capacity) — 200 more enrolled students over 10 years
  • Dropout reduction identified as the higher-leverage policy intervention
  • Quantitative basis for recommending student support over infrastructure investment
