Who I Am

I am an Information Systems postgraduate with a strong focus on designing practical, data-driven solutions to real-world problems. My work sits at the intersection of business and technology — I analyse challenges, design systems, and develop structured solutions that improve efficiency and decision-making.

I have hands-on experience in database design, system modelling, and digital platform development. My projects include a microfinance database system for a Bank of Uganda-regulated institution and a student-focused e-commerce platform serving the Kampala university market — applying both technical and business thinking to deliver functional and scalable solutions.
