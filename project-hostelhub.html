Overview

HostelHub Essentials is a B2C e-commerce platform built specifically for university students living in hostels and halls of residence in Kampala. The platform offers 32 curated products across 9 categories, all selected for small-space, budget-conscious living.

WordPress 6.x WooCommerce 9.x PHP / AJAX Yoast SEO UX Design Market Research

Problem

University students in Kampala — at Kyambogo, Makerere, and nearby institutions — spend significant time and money sourcing everyday essentials from busy markets like Kalerwe or roadside shops. The experience is fragmented, often overpriced for single items, and poorly matched to hostel realities: small rooms, shared spaces, limited budgets, and no transport.

No existing e-commerce platform in Uganda was purpose-built for this customer. Jumia and Kikuu carry everything for everyone — nobody was serving this specific group.

Solution

A niche B2C platform offering products specifically curated for hostel living — from bedding and kitchenware to personal care, study essentials, and a bundled Hostel Starter Kit at 26% discount. Free delivery within Kampala. Cash on Delivery to reduce purchase friction.
