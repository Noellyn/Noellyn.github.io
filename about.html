About Me

The person behind the work

Who I Am

I am an Information Systems postgraduate at Uganda Technology and Management University (UTAMU), Kampala, with a strong interest in solving real-world problems using technology and data. My work sits at the intersection of business needs and technical systems.

What I Am Good At

I think in systems. Whether I am designing a database schema, analysing a business process, or building a platform from scratch, I approach problems by understanding the whole before fixing the parts.

Systems thinking — I look for feedback loops, dependencies, and root causes rather than surface symptoms. Problem-solving — I have built a database system for a regulated financial institution, an e-commerce platform for a real market, and modelled complex systems using causal loop diagrams. Bridging business and IT — I speak both languages. I can gather requirements from non-technical stakeholders and translate them into structured technical solutions.

What I Am Looking For

Entry-level roles in Business Analysis, Systems Analysis, or data-related fields in Kampala — at banks, telcos, NGOs, government agencies, or technology companies. I am particularly interested in organisations that use data to make real decisions.
